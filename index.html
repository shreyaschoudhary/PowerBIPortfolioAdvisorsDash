Advisors Performance Dashboard

Project description: Dashboard Components 1. Advisor Roster Details - Tracks 56 advisors with a total monthly capacity of 772 GH assessments - Displays key metrics for each advisor: - Name, resource type (full-time/part-time) - Location (city), active programs (LI, GH, SH) - Maximum monthly GH assessment capacity - Shows concentration of advisors in Toronto and Richmond Hill areas 2. Regional Capacity & Utilization - Visualizes monthly assessment capacity vs. bookings across regions: - Toronto, Northern, Central, Eastern, Southwestern - Shows current utilization of GH advisors versus all scheduled assessments - Highlights Southwestern region with 586 of 772 maximum capacity utilized 3. Advisor Certification & Onboarding - Breakdown of 51 active advisors by: - Certification types (I-VI) - Program combinations (LI, GH, SH, SBP) - Geographic regions (Southern, Toronto, Eastern, Central) - Tracks 26 advisors in onboarding pipeline with certification details 4. Paperwork Submission Tracking - Monitors 3,707 scheduled assessments against 3,701 submissions - Identifies 6 outstanding paperwork items - Categorizes outstanding paperwork by assessment type (EAP Bill, SBP Initial) 5. Cancellation Analysis - Shows 19 cancellations due to max attempts (3%) - 265 no-shows (68% of cancellations) - Breakdown by zone (Central 218, Toronto 106) - Detailed cancellation reasons by advisor 6. Forecasting & Capacity Planning - Projects advisor availability and maximum assessments - Compares booked assessments vs remaining capacity - Regional forecasts showing Toronto with 2.1K booked of 6K capacity Key insights from this dashboard: - Advisor capacity utilization (772 max monthly capacity) - Paperwork compliance rate (99.8% submission rate) - Cancellation patterns (68% no-shows)

Skills used: Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling, Visualizations, SQL, SharePoint, Power Automate
