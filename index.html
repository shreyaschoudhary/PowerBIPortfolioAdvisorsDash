Project Overview

This dashboard was designed for a field operations team managing over 50 energy advisors responsible for completing sustainable energy assessments across Ontario. These assessments were critical for customers to access government rebates through programs such as Greener Homes (GH), Low Income (LI), and Small Business Programs (SBP). The dashboard provided an end-to-end operational view of advisor deployment, capacity, paperwork compliance, onboarding, and performance forecasting.

Key Objectives

  • Visualize advisor availability, workload, and GH monthly capacity
  • Track advisor onboarding and certification progress
  • Monitor scheduled vs. submitted assessment paperwork
  • Identify causes of appointment cancellations
  • Forecast remaining appointment capacity based on hours and region

Dashboard Highlights

Advisor Roster Details
- Real-time roster of 56 advisors
- Filterable by city, program, certification, and resource type
- Displays monthly GH assessment capacity per advisor

Regional Utilization Tracking
- Compares booked vs. available GH assessments by region
- Gauge visualization of total monthly capacity (772 GH/month)
- Highlights regions with under or over-utilization (e.g., Central, Toronto, Southwestern)

Advisor Certification & Onboarding
- Distinguishes active vs. onboarding advisors
- Tracks 26 onboarding advisors across various certification stages
- Visualized by program, region, and status (e.g., shadowing, documentation, QA pending)

Paperwork Submission Check
- Tracks scheduled vs. submitted paperwork weekly
- Displays outstanding items by advisor and assessment type
- Submission rate visualized (e.g., 3,701 out of 3,707 submitted = 99.8%)

Cancellation Analysis
- Categorizes cancellations: no-show, customer, resource, eligibility
- Trend lines showing cancellations per week
- Regional and advisor-specific breakdowns
- Identifies Central region and no-shows as top contributors

Assessment Duration Analysis
- Tracks average assessment duration by advisor
- Displays standard deviation and percentile benchmarks (50%, 70%, 90%)
- Highlights advisors who take significantly longer or shorter than average

Forecasting & Capacity Planning
- Projects how many assessments can still be booked
- Based on advisor hours, availability, and historical time per visit
- Regional forecasts (e.g., Toronto 2.1K booked vs 2.5K remaining)

Impact

  • Improved advisor utilization from ~68% to over 85%
  • Accelerated onboarding by identifying certification gaps
  • Achieved near-perfect paperwork compliance (99.8%)
  • Reduced cancellations and improved scheduling accuracy
  • Predicted capacity shortages before they occurred
